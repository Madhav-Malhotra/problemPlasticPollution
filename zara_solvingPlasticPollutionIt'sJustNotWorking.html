Solving plastic pollution - it’s just not working

April 20, 2020 • Posted by Zara Syed

We want to find all the reasons why solving plastic pollution isn't working.

A few weeks ago, we wanted to come up with a solution proposal that could potentially have a huge impact on the plastic pollution problem. What we’ve found is that at the root of everything, is a lack of economic incentives, and the solution is moving towards a circular economy. It seems every idea under the sun (including the ones we came up with) to help move towards a circular economy has been tried, and just isn’t enough. I changed my Slack status from “boutta solve plastic pollution” to “nvm, we can’t solve plastic pollution”. I added a “...yet” for a dash of optimism.

We still want to add value. Our new goal is to create + publish an extensive list of everything that’s happening and why it’s not working/won’t work. In the future, we hope that anyone wanting to solve this problem in the future can refer to this to know what not to do. Or take an existing solution, and know what to fix, since we’ve identified the short comings.

Coming up with all the reasons things aren’t working just seems to make the most sense if we want to add value. Coming up with a new solution... we’re likely to be another high potential idea that can’t scale. Maybe we can help ambitious people and companies put their time, money, and energy into more productive solutions.

- Zara
